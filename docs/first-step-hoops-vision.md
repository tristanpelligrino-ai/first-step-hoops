# First Step Hoops — Brand, Design System & Homepage Copy

---

# 1. Brand Overview

First Step Hoops is a youth basketball training program focused on helping young players build a strong foundation in the game.

The program emphasizes:
- Proper fundamentals
- Confidence-building
- Enjoyment of the game

This is not an elite or high-pressure training environment.  
It is designed to help kids **start the right way**.

---

# 2. Target Audience

## Primary Audience (Buyer)
Parents of 3rd–5th grade children who:
- Want their child to learn basketball correctly
- Value structure, coaching, and development
- Are not looking for elite or high-pressure training

## Secondary Audience (End User)
- Boys and girls (3rd–5th grade)
- All skill levels
- Beginners to early-stage players

---

# 3. Core Value Proposition

First Step Hoops helps kids:
- Understand how to play the game
- Develop proper fundamentals (footwork, shooting, passing, dribbling, finishing)
- Build confidence on the court
- Enjoy basketball and want to keep playing

---

# 4. Brand Personality

- Structured and disciplined
- Encouraging and supportive
- Energetic but controlled

The brand should feel:
- Trustworthy to parents
- Welcoming to kids
- Professional but approachable

---

# 5. Emotional Outcomes

## For Kids:
- Confidence
- Joy
- Passion for the game

## For Parents:
- Trust in coaching
- Confidence in development
- Comfort in environment

---

# 6. Brand Positioning

First Step Hoops is NOT:
- An elite training program
- A pipeline to college or professional basketball
- Focused on rankings, stats, or performance pressure
- Intimidating or overly intense

Instead, it is:
- A foundational training program
- Focused on learning the game the right way
- Designed for early development and enjoyment

---

## 7.2 Typography

### Headlines
- Bebas Neue / Oswald / Anton  

### Body
- Inter / Roboto / Open Sans  

---

## 7.3 Visual Style

- Clean and modern  
- Structured layout  
- Minimal clutter  
- Real youth training imagery  

Avoid:
- Flashy visuals  
- Neon overload  
- Elite/intense aesthetics  

---

## 7.4 Logo Direction

- Clean wordmark  
- Slight athletic feel  
- Subtle motion (step/forward progress)  
- No mascots
